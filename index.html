How To Play


Enter any number.

Mr. Roboger will respond by displaying numbers that begin at zero and chronologically count up to the number you enter.

But watch out! As Mr. Roboger counts, he likes to replace certain numbers with robo words and friendly questions.

The following rules will apply *

  • Numbers that contain a 1 will be replaced with "Beep!"
  • Numbers that contain a 2 will be replaced with "Boop!"
  • Numbers that contain a 3 will be replaced with "Won't you be my neighbor?"

* Numbers containing 3 are prioritized first, numbers containing 2 are prioritized next, and numbers containing 1 are prioritized last.
